HTML

HTML is a markup language used for creating the structure of the content on your webpage. Whether it be sections, paragraphs, links, elements, and tags. It's the skeleton of the website. No color, only structure

CSS

Cascading style sheets gives your webpage the splash of color needed to make everything look appealing to your users. CSS is used for positioning, color, fonts, and a little bit of animation. CSS brings your webpage to life.

JavaScript

JavaScript is an extremely versatile and powerful language that makes your website interactive. It brings your webpage to life, giving it functalitiy, such as advanced animations. It is one of the most used languages to date.

Responsive Design

Responsive design is a crucial part of creating a website. This is the practice of making your website render seamlessly on any device, such as tablets, phones, and desktops and ensures usability and functionality to the end user.
